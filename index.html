What Could Go Wrong?

"What Could Go Wrong?" explores a young woman plagued by anxiety as she attempts to buy a meaningful gift for her best friend, spiraling into an internal battle against self-doubt. For this project, I served as camera operator, editor, and screenwriter. I'm thrilled it won First Place at the Eagles Film Festival!

Sneakers

"Sneakers" is a four-minute horror-comedy about Jeremy, a Grade 11 student whose life spirals after finding mysterious sneakers on a trail. As camera operator and editor, I delivered the horror-comedy blend through dynamic camera movements and fast-paced, jarring editing to escalate tension. The film won Outstanding Achievement and Second Place at the Eagles Film Festival.
